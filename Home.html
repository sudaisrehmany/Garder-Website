Steps to start taking care of your plants

Humidity Control

You have to be diligent in finding out the temperature and humidity according to the type of plant have.

Pest Anticipation

Rotate crop types regularly by planting several different types of crops in one season.

Purning Weeds

Be diligent in cleaning the weeds or weeds that grow regularly to avoid pests and diseases.

Water properly

Water your plants according to their specific needs, avoiding overwatering or underwatering.

Why we love Gardens ?

Gardens provide us with a direct connection to the natural world, allowing us to experience its beauty and serenity.Gardens are visually pleasing, with their colorful flowers, lush greenery, and harmonious designs, appealing to our senses.Being in a garden can help reduce stress and promote relaxation, providing a peaceful escape from the demands of everyday life.Gardening itself can be a therapeutic activity, allowing us to engage with the earth, nurture plants, and experience a sense of accomplishment.Gardens symbolize growth, renewal, and the cycle of life, evoking a sense of optimism and hope.Gardens can be a reflection of our own creativity and personal style, allowing us to express ourselves through the arrangement and cultivation of plants.

Impact of Greenery on us

Greenery, such as trees and plants, helps improve our physical health. It contributes to cleaner air by absorbing carbon dioxide and releasing oxygen. Green spaces also help mitigate the urban heat island effect, providing shade and reducing temperatures.Being in the presence of greenery has been linked to positive psychological effects. It can reduce stress, anxiety, and symptoms of depression. Green environments promote relaxation, restoration, and a sense of calmness.Studies have shown that exposure to green spaces enhances cognitive function. It improves attention, concentration, and memory. Being in natural environments can also boost creativity and problem-solving abilities.
